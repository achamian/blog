How Would I Use Think-Center to Teach Riva Multiplication Tables

A thought experiment in parenting with AI perspectives

What's Think-Center?

Imagine having a council of advisors in your head - different perspectives that help you think through problems. I've been experimenting with this using AI. Today's simulation: multiplication tables.

The Challenge

Riva needs to learn her tables. I remember crying over 7√ó8. How do we break this cycle?

New chat, fresh context

What I Learned (In This Simulation)

  1. I was solving my trauma, not her problem - My impatience was creating the very stress I wanted to avoid
  2. Her "inefficient" method showed deep understanding - She wasn't slow, she was thorough
  3. My fear of her "falling behind" was inherited - From my parents, not from her reality

Now Riva Will Learn Her Tables and I Know...

  • Her methods are brilliant, not broken
  • Understanding beats memorization
  • My calm curiosity creates her confidence
  • Speed comes from comfort, not pressure
